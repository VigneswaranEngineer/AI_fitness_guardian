About Us:

At FitX, we understand the critical need for real-time, personalized health monitoring in today's fitness landscape. Too often, gym trainees lack comprehensive insights into their physiological parameters, leaving them vulnerable to injuries or suboptimal performance. That's why we've developed a groundbreaking system that combines state-of-the-art sensors with advanced AI algorithms to deliver unparalleled accuracy and customization.

Our Vision:

Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between generic fitness monitoring solutions and the dynamic needs of individuals engaged in intensive workout routines. Inspired by real-life tragedies like the untimely demise of fitness enthusiasts due to lack of personalized monitoring, we set out to create a solution that prioritizes user-specific data and intelligent feedback mechanisms.

How our System Works???

Our system employs a network of cutting-edge sensors, including DS18B20 for temperature, MAX30100 for pulse oximetry, and the Muscle Bio Amp Candy for muscle contractions, to capture and analyze key health indicators in real-time. Powered by the Raspberry Pi microprocessor, our centralized hub ensures efficient data processing and transmission, seamlessly connecting with our user-friendly mobile application via MQTT communication.

Integration with AI!!

What sets us apart is our integration of a Linear Regression model, a sophisticated AI algorithm that analyzes user-specific data accumulated over a 100-day period. This model establishes personalized thresholds for essential health metrics, enabling proactive alerts and personalized recommendations tailored to each user's unique fitness journey. With FitX, you'll have a virtual health companion at your fingertips.
